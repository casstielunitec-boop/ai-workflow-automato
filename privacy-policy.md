# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 18, 2026

## Overview

AI Workflow Automator is a Chrome extension that automates workflows across AI chatbot platforms. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

### What We Store (Local Only)
- Workflow configurations you create
- Optional API keys (Claude, OpenAI, Gemini)
- Usage statistics (anonymous)
- Execution history

### What We DON'T Do
- ❌ No external servers
- ❌ No data transmission to us
- ❌ No third-party sharing
- ❌ No tracking or analytics
- ❌ No cookies

## Data Storage

**All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.**

Your data never leaves your device:
- API keys are stored locally and used only for direct API calls
- Workflow data is stored in Chrome's localStorage
- No account registration required

## Third-Party Services

When using this extension, data is sent directly to AI platforms:
- OpenAI/ChatGPT
- Anthropic/Claude
- Google/Gemini
- Other AI platforms you configure

Please refer to each platform's privacy policy for their data handling practices.

## Permissions

Our extension uses these permissions:
- **storage**: Save workflows locally
- **activeTab**: Access AI platform pages
- **scripting**: Automate workflow execution
- **tabs**: Manage multiple AI platform tabs

## Your Rights

You have full control:
- View all stored data anytime
- Delete data by uninstalling the extension
- Export your workflows
- No data retention after uninstall
